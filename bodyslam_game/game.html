Hello and welcome to Champions of Wrestling!!!

The Brooklyn Brawler knows 4 moves:
bodyslam: An attack that pumps you up for your next attack! +4 to hit, d8+1 to damage, +2 to next attack on a hit
clothesline: A devastating attack, but it lands less often. +4 to hit, d12+4 to damage
suplex: His signature move, but you can only use it once a match. +4 to hit, 2d12+4 damage
DDT: The most devastating move the Brawler knows, but it can only be used 1 time! +4 to hit, 3d12+4 damage

Movement: You can do 1 move action per turn.
One must be engaged to do grapple moves. One must be disengaged to do running moves.
(Note: All of the Brooklyn Brawler's moves are currently grapple moves)
If you have moved on your turn before attacking, your attack will include a -1 penalty
