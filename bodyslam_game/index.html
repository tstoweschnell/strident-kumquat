Hello and welcome to Champions of Wrestling!!!

The Brooklyn Brawler knows 4 moves:
bodyslam: A more accurate move, but causes less damage.
clothesline: A devastating attack, but it lands less often.
suplex: His signature move, but you can only use it once a match
DDT: The most devastating move the Brawler knows, but it can only be used 1 time!

Movement: You can do 1 move action per turn.
One must be engaged to do grapple moves. One must be disengaged to do running moves.
(Note: All of the Brooklyn Brawler's moves are currently grapple moves)
If you have moved on your turn before attacking, your attack will include a -1 penalty
